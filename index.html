▧UX Writing

As a longtime community member of the visual research tool Are.na, I was really excited to lead an overhaul of the platform's help documentation timed with the release of their new web client. The project included a full restructuring of their information architecture within a new content management system, revising and expanding the copy along for new instructional articles and resources.

▧Data Storytelling

I collaborated in-house with market researchers at Statista, covering global energy trade, renewable resources, and Latin American agriculture. My main task was writing and editing a high volume of industry reports for B2B audiences to provide narrative context and searchable text content for data visualizations and graphs.
