Morgan Mansour

I am a writer, educator, and creative developer focused on experimental publishing and technical documentation.

Current Projects

⏵Expanding poetry websites into sound art & performance

I make poem-websites by shaping the writing and interface as a holistic process. I take a visual and sonic approach to the text, working simultaneously between code, audio, and design tools. The most recent piece was designed as a mobile website and reinterpreted into a poetry reading at HFBK Hamburg, where I am studying a master's with the Klasse Digitale Grafik. I am now developing a site that emerges from audio snippets, and am learning to incorporate sound tools and more musical elements into the live readings.

⏵Looking for freelance work in UX & technical writing

I'm seeking out projects related to information architecture, usability design, and technical content strategy. Past experience includes a full restructuring of the help docs for Are.na, timed with the release of a new web client. I've also worked on the editorial research team at Statista, where I wrote and edited industry reports to accompany infographics on market data. A background in language and media education has been formative in how I think about writing informational content and supporting the reader at each step.

⏵Growing communities, teaching, & organizing

As of spring 2026, I am co-founding a cooking collective hosted at my university, in tandem with a poetic web project on gardening and culinary practices. Since winter 2025, I have also been building a creative computing/libre graphics club in Hamburg with fellow organizers of Hamburg HTML Day, planned as part of a worldwide event. In summer 2025, I also led a daylong workshop on net art as a guest lecturer in a design foundations course at Burg Halle.
