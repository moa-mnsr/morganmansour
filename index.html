●art & design projects

This winter, I finished a new mobile site with four fresh poems to accompany a live reading. Last fall, I started a Master of Fine Arts at HFBK Hamburg with the Klasse Digitale Grafik.

This spring, I will be co-creating an IRL cooking & gardening collective at HFBK Hamburg, alongside an ephemeral online field guide.

●freelance tech writing

From the spring on, I am looking for projects in UX & technical writing. Past work includes a full overhaul of the help docs for Are.na, and writing industry reports on market data at Statista.

A background in language and media education has strongly shaped my informational writing to support the reader at each step.

●teaching & organizing

Last summer, I guest taught a daylong workshop about net art in a design class at Burg Halle. I also hosted a local HTML Day in Hamburg as part of a worldwide event, and have been organizing a local creative computing/libre graphics club ever since. I am always excited for the chance to teach, so feel free to reach out!

●upcoming book collab

Starting this summer, I will be writing a book about poetic computation and a DIY coding framework called Puffins, made by London-based programmer Mat Hill. Watch this space for more info soon!
