Current Projects

⏵Expanding poetry websites into sound art & performance

I make poem-websites by shaping the writing and interface as a holistic process. I take a visual and sonic approach to the text, working simultaneously between code, audio, and design tools. The most recent piece was conceived as a mobile website to accompany a live reading at an exhibition I helped design at the HFBK Hamburg, where I am currently studying a master's with the Klasse Digitale Grafik. Next I want to work on a site that emerges from audio snippets, and slowly begin to incorporate sound technology and more musical elements into the readings.

⏵Looking for freelance work in UX & technical writing

I'd be excited to work with small startups on information architecture, technical content strategy, user guides and interface copy. Past experience includes a full restructuring of the help docs for the visual research platform Are.na, where I had been a longtime user and community member. The project included an overhaul of their copy and user resources within a new content management system, and was timed with the release of a new web client. I've also worked with the editorial team at Statista, where I wrote and edited a high volume of industry reports on global energy markets, renewable resources, and agriculture to improve SEO for their infographics.

⏵Growing communities, teaching, & organizing

As of spring 2026, I am working on a collective dinner project that accompanies a poetic field guide to vegetables, gardening and culinary practices, and simultaneously building a Creative Computing/Libre Graphics club in Hamburg. In summer 2025, I taught a daylong workshop as a guest in a design foundations course at Burg Halle, and organized Hamburg HTML Day as part of a worldwide event. Past work includes creative code courses for young learners and many years in language education.
