I am a writer and web designer working in experimental publishing.

●artistic projects

I make poem-websites by shaping the text and interface as whole. I take a visual and sonic approach to the writing process, working simultaneously between code, audio, and design tools.

The most recent piece The End The End The End was designed as a mobile website and reinterpreted into a live reading. A compilation of earlier sites made up my portfolio to study a master's with the Klasse Digitale Grafik at HFBK Hamburg.

I am currently collaborating on an ephemeral online field guide to vegetables with Veronika Pluzhnikova, and a book about poetic computation with Mat Hill.

●teaching & organizing

In summer 2025, I guest taught a daylong workshop about net art in a design foundations class at Burg Halle, and hosted a local HTML Day in Hamburg as part of a worldwide event. Since the start of 2026, I have been working on organizing a local creative computing club in Hamburg.

I am always excited for the chance to teach and to collaborate, so feel free to reach out!

●editorial work

Drawing from a background as a language and media educator, I specialize in writing informational content such as product documentation and data reports. I am currently looking for freelance projects in UX & technical writing.

Past work includes a full restructuring of the copy and information architecture of the help docs for Are.na, and in-house writing and editing at the market research platform Statista. CV and samples are available on request.
