I am a writer and artist making poem-websites, friendly documentation, and some things in between.

●studies & art practice

This winter, I finished a new mobile site with four fresh poems to accompany a live reading. Last fall, I started a Master of Fine Arts at HFBK Hamburg with the Klasse Digitale Grafik.

This spring, I will be co-creating an IRL cooking & gardening collective at HFBK Hamburg, alongside an ephemeral online field guide.

●freelance tech writing

From the spring and on, I am looking for projects in UX & technical writing. Past work includes a full overhaul of the help docs for Are.na, and writing industry reports on market data at Statista.

●upcoming book collab

Starting this summer, I will be writing a book about poetic computation and a DIY coding framework called Puffins, made by London-based programmer Mat Hill. Watch this space for more info soon!

●ongoing teaching work

I have been building a creative computing/libre graphics club in Hamburg since hosting a local HTML Day last summer. Around the same time, I also guest taught a daylong workshop about net art in a design class at Burg Halle.
