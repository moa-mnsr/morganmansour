Projects

⏵Design & Code

I make hybrid poem-websites that take the frontend and writing as a holistic process. I take a visual and sonic approach to composing the text, working simultaneously between VS Code, Audacity, and Figma. This collection of pieces from 2023 to 2025 served as my portfolio to study with the Klasse Digitale Grafik and develop into a master's project.

⏵UX Writing

As a longtime user and community member of the visual research tool Are.na, I was truly thrilled to lead an overhaul of the platform's help documentation timed with the release of their new web client. The project included a full restructuring of their information architecture within a new content management system, revising and expanding the copy into new instructional articles and resources.

⏵Data Storytelling

I collaborated in-house with market researchers at Statista, covering global energy trade, renewable resources, and Latin American agriculture. My main task was writing and editing a high volume of industry reports for B2B audiences to provide narrative context and searchable text content for data visualizations and graphs.

⏵Community Growth

Bringing a background in language education, I teach creative code and net art through the lens of media literacy. Recently, I guest taught in a Kommunikationsdesign foundations workshop at Burg Halle, and organized Hamburg HTML Day as part of a worldwide event. Past work includes community development at SuperHi and computational arts courses with MAMA.codes.
