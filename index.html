I am a writer and educator making poem-websites, friendly documentation, and some things in between.

●art & design projects

This winter, I finished a new mobile site with four fresh poems to accompany a live reading at HFBK Hamburg, where I started a master's last fall. A compilation of past poem-sites made up my portfolio to study with the Klasse Digitale Grafik, and can be found in The Internet Phonebook. This spring, I will work on incorporating sound art into sites & performances. I am also collaborating on an ephemeral online field guide to vegetables while starting a cooking & gardening collective.

●freelance tech writing

From the spring on, I am looking for projects in UX & technical writing. Past work includes a full restructuring of the copy and information architecture of the help docs at Are.na, and writing short reports to support data literacy and search visibility for the market research platform, Statista. CV available on request.

●teaching & organizing

Last summer, I guest taught a daylong workshop about net art in a design class at Burg Halle. I also hosted a local HTML Day in Hamburg as part of a worldwide event, and have been organizing a local creative computing/libre graphics club ever since. I am always excited for the chance to teach, so feel free to reach out!

●upcoming book collab

Starting this summer, I will be writing a book about poetic computation and a DIY coding framework called Puffins, made by London-based programmer Mat Hill. Watch this space for more info soon!
