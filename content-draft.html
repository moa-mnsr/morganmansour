Bringing a background in language education, I approach creative code instruction through the lens of media literacy. Recently, I organized HTML Day in Hamburg as part of a worldwide event and joined Anna Unterstab for a guest teaching day in her Kommunikationsdesign foundations course at Burg Halle. I’ve also taught computational thinking and DIY web design for kids at MAMA.codes, and worked in educational community development for adult learners at SuperHi. During the pandemic, I co-founded the remote creative practice group There Is Only Make, where I began to experiment with interactive designs for poem sites, and was inspired by group Sunday Sites, where I started to learn to code as an early member.
